About This Collection
This collection features interactive physics simulations created by Eunoia Junior College teachers for the 2026 Singapore-Cambridge A Level Physics syllabus. Each simulation is designed to help students visualize complex concepts and develop deeper understanding through hands-on exploration.

Teachers tag simulations with the appropriate level(s), so you'll find simulations organized by H1, H2, or H3 Physics. Many simulations are suitable for multiple levels.
